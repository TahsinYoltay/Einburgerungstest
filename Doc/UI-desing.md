Design Documentation: Life in UK Exam App - Clean & Professional Theme
Overall Design Philosophy: The application follows a "Clean & Professional" aesthetic, characterized by a light theme, subtle blue gradients, and a clear, organized layout. Emphasis is placed on intuitive navigation, readable typography, ample whitespace, and interactive elements that enhance the user's study experience.

Color Palette:

Primary Background: Light, clean whites/off-whites.
Accent Colors: Various shades of blue for gradients, interactive elements, progress bars, and call-to-action buttons.
Text: Dark gray for primary text, lighter gray for secondary information.
Icons: Consistent use of line-art or filled icons in shades of blue or dark gray.
Typography:

Headings/Titles: Clear, legible sans-serif font (e.g., Roboto, Open Sans) in medium to bold weights.
Body Text: Same sans-serif font, regular weight, optimized for readability.
Interactive Elements (Buttons, Labels): Consistent font, often slightly bolder for emphasis.
Common UI Elements:

Cards: Used extensively for grouping related content, providing clear visual separation, and enabling interactive elements (e.g., exam entries, progress summaries).
Progress Bars: Clean, horizontal bars with blue fill to indicate completion status.
Icons: Intuitive, universally recognized icons used to support labels and actions.
Buttons: Clearly defined, often rectangular with rounded corners, using blue accents for primary actions.

Context: Main entry point for users to access various exam preparation modes and track overall progress.

Top Section: Quick stats dashboard displaying overall progress, possibly with a summary or key metrics.

Clear title and descriptive text.
Progress bar or completion indicator.
Relevant icon.
Call-to-Action: Prominent button for "Mock Exams" or similar high-priority action.
Navigation: Mobile navigation bar at the bottom with consistent icons: ["Home", "Study", "Exams", "Progress", "Settings"].
2. Book Section Exam Screen (Clean & Professional - Detailed Chapters & Sections)
Screen ID: ada9e2298be1492a8102cb9e36354c72
Context: Allows users to study and practice by individual book chapters and their respective sections.
Layout:
Chapter List: A vertical list or grid of 6 chapters. Each chapter acts as an expandable element.
Collapsed Chapter Card: Displays chapter title, overall chapter progress (e.g., using a progress bar).
Expanded Chapter Card: Reveals a nested list of individual sections within that chapter.
Each section item includes its title, specific section progress/status, and an action button (e.g., "Start Practice").
Interaction: Tapping a chapter expands/collapses its sections. Tapping a section initiates practice.
Navigation: Mobile navigation bar at the bottom with consistent icons: ["Home", "Study", "Exams", "Progress", "Settings"].

4. Past Exams Screen (Clean & Professional - Redesigned for Clarity)
Screen ID: 6536ec2fb48c410aa3384370f0bfa96c
Context: Displays a history of user's past exams, overall performance, and options to review or retake tests.
Layout:
Top Progress Card: A prominent, spacious card at the top showing:
"Overall Progress" title.
"Average Score" percentage.
"Completed Test Progress" percentage.
Insight Cards (Horizontal): Two distinct, well-separated horizontal cards below the progress card:
"Wrong Answered" (count/percentage).
"Restarted Questions" (count).
Practice Test List: A scrollable main section listing approximately 40 "Practice Test" entries (e.g., Exam 1, Exam 2, etc.).
Collapsed Exam Card: Displays exam name and a summary (e.g., "Last Attempted").
Expanded Exam Card: When tapped, expands to reveal:
"Last Score".
"Total Number of Questions".
Contextual action buttons: "Start", "Restart", or "Retake Exam" (depending on test status).
Clarity: Ample whitespace throughout to prevent text overcrowding. Clear visual hierarchy for all text and interactive elements.
Navigation: Mobile navigation bar at the bottom with consistent icons: ["Home", "Study", "Exams", "Progress", "Settings"].
5. Favorite Questions (Swipe Review) Screen
Screen ID: 4b22a16690b94683a7faa983cee2c124
Context: Provides an interactive, swipe-based interface for reviewing favorite multiple-choice questions.
Layout:
Central Question Card: A large, prominent card filling the majority of the screen.
Top: Displays the multiple-choice question text clearly.
Bottom: Lists ABC/ABCD answer options below the question text.
Swipe Interaction: The card should support left/right swiping gestures to navigate to the next/previous favorited question.
Action Buttons (Below Card): Three distinct and easily accessible buttons:
"Hint" (icon + text): Reveals a hint for the question.
"Explanation" (icon + text): Displays the full explanation for the question.
"Result" (icon + text): Shows the correct answer and performance.
Clarity: Focus on clean typography for questions and options, with sufficient line spacing.
Navigation: Mobile navigation bar at the bottom with consistent icons: ["Home", "Study", "Exams", "Progress", "Settings"].
